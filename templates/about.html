About XeroWaste

XeroWaste is an AI-powered Smart Kitchen & Waste Minimizer system designed for restaurants. Our hackathon project aims to revolutionize how restaurants manage inventory, reduce food waste, and increase profitability through cutting-edge technology.

Our system uses computer vision, machine learning, and advanced analytics to automate inventory tracking, predict food spoilage, optimize menus, and deliver actionable insights.

Dashboard Features:

  • Comprehensive inventory management system
  • Detailed waste tracking and categorization
  • Real-time stock monitoring with expiry date alerts
  • Data visualization and reporting tools

Our Mission

We're on a mission to reduce food waste in the restaurant industry through innovative AI technology. By helping restaurants optimize their operations, we aim to create a more sustainable and profitable food service ecosystem.

Dashboard Preview

Main Dashboard

Our main dashboard provides a complete overview of your restaurant's inventory status, including:

  • Total product count (16 in the example)
  • Current stock levels across all categories
  • Expired items alert (6 items in the example)
  • Total waste monitoring
  • Recent inventory additions with expiry tracking

Inventory Management

The inventory management screen shows:

  • Product details organized by categories
  • Current stock quantities for each product
  • Expiry dates with visual indicators
  • Price tracking over the last 15 days
  • Quick actions for editing or deleting items

Waste Tracking

Our waste tracking system enables:

  • Detailed waste categorization (Plastic, Metal, etc.)
  • Date-based filtering and organization
  • Source tracking to identify problem areas
  • Quantity measurement in appropriate units
  • Easy addition of new waste entries

How to Access the Dashboard

You can access the dashboard by clicking on the "Log in" button in the navigation menu and entering your credentials. After logging in, you will be automatically redirected to the dashboard page where you can view all the features described above.

Hackathon Project

Challenge Statement:

"Create an AI-powered Smart Kitchen System for Restaurants that uses computer vision and machine learning to automate inventory tracking, predict food spoilage, and optimize operations to minimize waste and maximize efficiency."

Our Approach:

  • Computer Vision for Smart Inventory Management
  • AI-Powered Demand & Waste Prediction
  • Intelligent Menu Optimization
  • Vision-Powered Waste Analysis & Reporting

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: React (Web)
  • Backend: Node.js (Express)
  • Database: PostgreSQL & MongoDB
  • AI/ML Frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV
  • Vision APIs: Google Vision, AWS Rekognition
